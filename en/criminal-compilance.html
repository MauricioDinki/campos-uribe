Campos, Campos & Uribe we are one of the pioneering law firms in Mexico in Criminal Compliance. The firm has trained and certified personnel in foreign universities promoting the culture of prevention in criminal matters..

In addition, Campos, Campos & Uribe is one of the firms that has internally implemented a criminal compliance program, which represents its commitment to the promotion and implementation of a corporate culture of prevention..

Phases of implementation of a criminal compliance program:

Risk analysis and identification

Controls for the mitigation of criminal risks

Code of Ethics

Complaint Channel

Evaluation, training, and staff development

Support for the appointment of an internal or external risk assessment and monitoring officer.

Among the most relevant crimes that can be committed by a company are:

Tax fraud

Money laundering

Corruption acts

Bribery

Influence peddling

Discrimination

Crimes against the environment
