To carry out the purposes described in this privacy notice, we will use the following personal data: full name, age; sex; home; private telephone and email; where appropriate, autograph signature; Photography; current job; Work phone number and email.

In addition to the personal data mentioned in the immediately preceding paragraph, for the fulfillment of the purposes of this Privacy Notice, the following personal data considered sensitive may be required, which require special protection: diseases, allergies, practicing religion, political position , sexual preference, economic capacity, financial information, among others.

If necessary, your personal data will be shared within or outside the national territory only to achieve the purposes of the legal services for which "CAMPOS, CAMPOS & URIBE" has been hired.

7. RIGHTS OF ACCESS, RECTIFICATION, CANCELLATION AND OPPOSITION (ARC)

The Holder has the right to know what personal data we have, in what we use them for, and the conditions of the use we give them (Access). It is also your right to request the correction of your personal information if it is outdated, inaccurate or incomplete (Rectification); that we remove it from our records or databases when it considers that it is not being used in accordance with the principles, duties and obligations set forth in the regulations (Cancellation); as well as oppose the use of your personal data for specific purposes (Opposition). These rights are known as ARCO rights.

For the exercise of any of the ARCO rights, you must submit the respective application in the format that will be provided for this purpose at the express request of the Holder and within a period not exceeding 10 business days attention will be given to it.

8. REVOCATION OF CONSENT

The Holder may revoke the consent that, in his case, he has granted us for the treatment of his personal data. However, it is important that you keep in mind that not in all cases we will be able to attend your request or terminate the use immediately, since it is possible that for some legal obligation we require to continue treating your personal data. Likewise, you should consider that for certain purposes, the revocation of your consent will imply that we cannot continue to provide the service you requested, or the conclusion of your relationship with us.

The revocation procedure must be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the immediately preceding paragraph.
